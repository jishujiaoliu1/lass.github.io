What do you think is the fun of writing code ?
==
When I saw the purple Merge icon, I finally felt it——It is not a waste of time to revise PR and communicate with maintainers again and again;The computer knowledge I learned in the past few years was not for idleness or self-entertainment;The code development projects I have written in recent years are not done behind closed doors and for self-admiration;Although I will get old one day, the code will remain in the project and continue to run;Although my abilities are limited, I have indeed made a small contribution to developers around the world;Although my influence is limited, I can make my code run on servers and future projects all over the world;This purest sense of achievement is difficult to obtain in other aspects.

What do you think of the phenomenon that programmers copy code and still get high salaries?
==

It reminded me of a time when I went to an embedded system company for an interview and there were more than 20 people.Seven programming questions, paper version. You are required to write the code on the computer, without supervision. You can even use gpt to search the code.After three hours of work, I gave it to the interviewer for assessment. At first, I didn't know why those people came back dejected, until they knew I entered the interview room.Tell me about your code, I thought it was so easy. After I finished, the interviewer pointed at a code question (the most standard quick sort without any variants), and said that he deleted a few lines and asked me to fill them in on the spot.I said no need to look up the information, I can fix it after taking a look. After spending a few minutes to fix the code, it was exactly the same as the original one.Didn't say much, just said to change people to discuss salary and job content.When I got to the recruitment office, the HR told me that no less than 80 people had come in the past few days, and all of them had been asked to delete the code and could not fix it. The interviewer lowered the requirements and said that it was not necessary to write very precisely, just to say what needed to be fixed, where a few lines were missing, and what these lines were used for.This is it, it stumped a lot of people. I think any medium-level Leecode question is much easier than this.I can only say that you can't imagine the lower limit of computers.
